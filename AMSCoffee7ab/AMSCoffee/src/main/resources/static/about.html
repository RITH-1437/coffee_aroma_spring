Our Story

Brewing excellence since 2025

Where It All Began

Founded in 2025 by passionate coffee enthusiasts Maria and Carlos Rodriguez, Café Aroma began as a small neighborhood coffee shop with a big dream: to bring people together through exceptional coffee experiences.

What started with a single espresso machine and a handful of loyal customers has grown into a beloved community hub where friends gather, students study, and coffee lovers discover their perfect cup.

Today, we continue to uphold our founding principles: quality ingredients, expert craftsmanship, and genuine hospitality that makes everyone feel at home.

Our Mission & Values

What drives us every day

Our Mission

To create memorable coffee experiences that bring people together, support our community, and celebrate the art of exceptional coffee making. We are committed to sustainability, quality, and fostering connections one cup at a time.

Sustainability

We source our beans ethically and implement eco-friendly practices to protect our planet for future generations.

Quality

Every bean is carefully selected and every cup is crafted with precision to ensure the highest quality experience.

Community

We believe in building strong relationships with our customers, suppliers, and the local community we serve.
